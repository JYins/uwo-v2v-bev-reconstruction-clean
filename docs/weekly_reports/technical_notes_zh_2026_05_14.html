  1. I first revisited Diffusion and made sure the comparison was valid.
  2. Diffusion v3 became a complete negative baseline: high recall but very low precision.
  3. I tested a teacher-inspired registered/pre-layer Diffusion follow-up.
  4. The follow-up still showed saturation, so I decided not to waste more GPU on blind reruns.
  5. I redesigned the dataset into dataset_v2 with multiple noisetypes and preprocessing metadata.
  6. I corrected the training protocol to use one mixed-noise dataset, not separate small datasets.
  7. Mixed U-Net and Pix2Pix training are now queued/running on Narval.
  8. I also set up the paper-writing workflow to connect literature review, notes, experiments, and thesis chapters.
